AT Nearby
UX/UI & Front End

AT Explore Nearby will be an app designed to allow users to find destinations based on their location. The app is designed for Auckland Transport to help promote businesses around Auckland and also promote public transport by helping the user get to those locations. The app doesn’t have a search function as we want users to discover and find new destinations rather than search for them.

Belly Worship
UX/UI & Front End

Belly Worship digital ordering kiosk system using a table side mounted tablet. The system will have an eftpos facility built into it for easy payment, if cash payment is required users can go up to the counter and the restaurant staff can easy pull up their table and individual order thanks to the personalised ordering system. Users will have the ability to sync their personal devices to the ordering system by entering the table number and selecting their name.
